Our Projects

Crowd impact

A digital, decentralized crowdfunding platform that enables CrowdImpact verified projects and early stage NGOs to post their empowerment and social enterprise projects and source for impact funding from a network of both corporates (through CSR), individual and donors on CrowdImpact Seed Crowdfunding Platform.


Tubonge App

A Mobile App that utilize a power of AI and creative contents (Animation, videos, audio in podcast and graphical presentation in info graphs and motion pictures) to provide education and awareness in sexual reproductive health and rights to underserved communities. The APP also allows users to initiate topic and contribute to topics in a Tubonge forum where they will be meet different SRHR stakeholders and engage with them. Additionally the APP is integrated with a digital real time communication function that user can reachout to professionals and experts in SRHR and get the help they need.


Project Tumaini

The project aims at empowering people living with HIV/AIDS economic well being and status, whereby the project intends to primary benefits people living with AIDS (PLWA) who are unemployed but have the passion to work and contribute to raise economic status of their families, it will also benefit all those who want to develop their businesses, build up their own ventures and their incomes. We will initiate this project by providing mentorship to PLWA in entrepreneurship activities. Through this project, we will strengthen their economic activities through entrepreneurship training and business development support also help connect them with financial institutions for funding as well as connect them with market.


Sauti ya Mnyonge

This project aims at mobilizing the underprivileged group and form a strong unity with a mutual loud voice that presents their views to better access of different services and resources through arts (music, dance, painting, videography, posters, flyers, comics, you name it). The primary focus will be on; Access to quality healthy services, education services, Access to decent work, Gender equality, Access to leadership opportunities, inclusion in decision making as well as economic empowerment.


Kituturi Program

Is a fully capacity building program that offer platform that provided youth essential skills in organizational development and team management as well as hints on how to find and get useful information that touches their lives either directly or indirectly i.e social, economic and political affairs and what are their roles in bringing the positive changes in the community through engaging and participating effectively in education, health, empowerment for PLWD, poverty reduction, food security and gender issues. The platform will also create a room for networking and partnership among youth fostering transformation through interactions among the participants by implementing projects born from their interaction in consortium.
