About us

We are advocacy, autonomous, non-partisan and non-government organization established in 2020 and registered in 2021 under the Non-governmental organization Act, 2002 with registration No. 00NGO/R/1637. We have been registered and accredited to operate all over Tanzania, but we are currently based in Dar es salaam as our main office and in Mwanza as our branch. The purpose of the organization is to promote economic growth through facilitating entrepreneurship capacity building, advocating for youth inclusion and gender sensitivity in economic growth in Tanzania as well as liaise with both local and international organizations to implement gender and entrepreneurship development

> projects.

Our Thematic Area

  • Empowerment of Communities
    • To contribution towards national economic growth through job creation, entrepreneurship and business development support.
  • Gender
    • To advance gender equality by ending all form of inequalities towards adolescent girls and young women in the communities through creative innovative empowerment interventions.
  • Agriculture
    • To enhance crop productivity and market performance in Tanzania by providing extension services, market linkage and solve post-harvesting losses to farmers.
  • Education
    • To ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all by eliminating all forms of learning poverty.

Our Vision

To build an innovative and learning society that is free from youth unemployment, gender disparity and achieve inclusive economic growth across all sectors in Tanzania. 

Our Mission

To facilitate quality gender-responsive entrepreneurship and business development programs through institutional engagements and collaborations with both private and public sectors. 

Our Objectives

  • To promote economic growth by conducting entrepreneurship trainings and business development programs for potential and existing entrepreneurs.
