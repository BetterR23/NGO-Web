About us

We are advocacy, autonomous, non-partisan and non-government organization established in 2020 and registered in 2021 under the Non-governmental organization Act, 2002 with registration No. 00NGO/R/1637. We have been registered and accredited to operate all over Tanzania, but we are currently based in Dar es salaam as our main office and in Mwanza as our branch. The purpose of the organization is to promote economic growth through facilitating entrepreneurship capacity building, advocating for youth inclusion and gender sensitivity in economic growth in Tanzania as well as liaise with both local and international organizations to implement gender and entrepreneurship development projects.

Our Vision

To build an innovative and learning society that is free from youth unemployment, gender disparity and achieve inclusive economic growth across all sectors in Tanzania. 

Our Mission

To facilitate quality gender-responsive entrepreneurship and business development programs through institutional engagements and collaborations with both private and public sectors. 

Our Objectives

  • To promote economic growth by conducting entrepreneurship trainings and business development programs for potential and existing entrepreneurs.


  • To advance gender equality by reducing inequalities towards adolescent girls and young women in the communities through creative innovative empowerment interventions


  • To support and undertake research on gender inclusion in the economic sector by involving government, donors and researchers.


  • To advance public education and awareness through dialogues, discussions and debates on youth economic engagement and gender-sensitive issues.
